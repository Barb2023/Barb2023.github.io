Skill set as Business Data Analyst

Key skills Description
Structured Query Language We focus on DML (Data Manipulation Language) where we build queries to satisfy aggregations, segments, and align to business strategies. We also cover reporting data structure design, optimization, and maintenance using DDL (Data Definition Language).
Data Reporting Using MSExcel/Advance excel, Power Query ,SSIS ,Tableau, pulling data using SQL and Power BI tools.
Business Intelligence Can create, implement, and manage an enterprise BI solution. Topics covered include modelling, monitoring, and analytics capability. To build reports that foster informed business decisions. Solutions implement security and collaboration with communications utilities and mobile devices. We learned to cover preparation of reports using relational and dimensional data, and will use BI tools to perform statistical analysis.
Business Analysis Knows fundamental business processes that govern the operation of any business. The focus is on the tools and methodologies used by business analysts to gather information regarding those operational processes and how the gathered information is used to support strategic and tactical decision-making in the business. This fundamental understanding of the business and analysis is then used to accurately interpret the detailed business requirements that drive design and implementation of supporting IT products and services.
Database Design Knows how database work and design databaase. Learned what is affecting performance from hardware, networking, security, platform and architectural points of view . Being aware of the importance of fast, reliable access to data in all organizational areas.
Power BI Dashboard Maintaining and updating Power BI dashboards, connecting to various data sources, transforming and cleaning data, and managing data refresh schedules. Performance optimization, security implementation, regular updates, and effective collaboration and sharing with stakeholders ensure that essential accurate, up-to-date, and insightful reports are provided..
Knowledge on data Provisioning with ETL Designing the data flow for data migration of existing or new records to be uploaded.
