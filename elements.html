Business Intelligence & Analytics

Courses

Subjects Description
Structured Query Language This course focus on DML (Data Manipulation Language) where students will build queries to satisfy aggregations, segments, and align to business strategies. Students also cover reporting data structure design, optimization, and maintenance using DDL (Data Definition Language). 1st sem
Data Reporting Using MSExcel/Advance excel and Power Query. 1st sem
Introduction to Business Intelligence Be able to create, implement, and manage an enterprise BI solution. Topics covered include modelling, monitoring, and analytics capability. To build reports that foster informed business decisions. Solutions implement security and collaboration with communications utilities and mobile devices. We learned to cover preparation of reports using relational and dimensional data, and will use BI tools to perform statistical analysis. 1st sem
Business Analysis Essensials Knows fundamental business processes that govern the operation of any business. The focus is on the tools and methodologies used by business analysts to gather information regarding those operational processes and how the gathered information is used to support strategic and tactical decision-making in the business. This fundamental understanding of the business and analysis is then used by the learners to accurately interpret the detailed business requirements that drive design and implementation of supporting IT products and services. Extensive use is made of real world case studies from the local area, including guest lectures from local business analysts. Tools and software used in the course will be chosen according to current local industry standards.. 1st sem
Database Design Knowing how database works and do database design. Learned what is affecting performance from hardware, networking, security, platform and architectural points of view . Being aware of the importance of fast, reliable access to data in all organizational areas. 1st sem
